# YouTube publication checklist

## Files

- Video: `../video/ragdoll-build-week-demo.mp4`
- Thumbnail: `../video/youtube-thumbnail.png`
- Selectable caption track: `../video/ragdoll-build-week-demo.srt`
- Copy: this directory's `YOUTUBE-DESCRIPTION.md`

## Upload

1. Open YouTube Studio and choose **Create → Upload videos**.
2. Select `ragdoll-build-week-demo.mp4`.
3. Paste the title and description from `YOUTUBE-DESCRIPTION.md`.
4. Upload `youtube-thumbnail.png` as the custom thumbnail.
5. Choose **No, it is not made for kids** and leave paid promotion disabled.
6. Under **Subtitles**, add English and upload `ragdoll-build-week-demo.srt` as the selectable
   caption track.
7. Set visibility to **Public**, not Private or Unlisted.
8. Wait for 1080p processing, then watch the public video once with sound.
9. Copy the public `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...` URL into the Devpost video field.

## Final checks

- Runtime shown by YouTube remains below three minutes.
- The video plays publicly while signed out.
- Audio is understandable and captions are synchronized.
- The description and disclosure do not claim a successful live GPT-5.6 run.
- Repository and documentation links resolve.
